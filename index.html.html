YOLO V8
Real Time Detection of Falls Among Elderly People Using YOLOv8

Falls among the elderly population represent a significant global health concern, often leading to serious injuries, loss of independence, and increased mortality. Early and accurate detection of fall events in real time is therefore critical for timely medical intervention and improved quality of care. This project presents a real-time fall detection system for elderly individuals using YOLOv8, one of the most advanced object detection architectures available today. • The proposed system leverages the YOLOv8 model trained on a custom dataset comprising annotated video frames depicting various fall scenarios and normal daily activities. The dataset includes diverse environments such as living rooms, corridors, and outdoor spaces to ensure robust generalization. Data augmentation techniques including flipping,rotation,brightnessadjustment, and mosaic augmentation were applied to improve model performance. • Experimental results show that the proposed system outperforms conventional approaches in terms of detection speed and accuracy. The system is capable of processing video streams in real time with minimal latency, making it deployable in smart home environments, hospitals, and elderly care facilities.
